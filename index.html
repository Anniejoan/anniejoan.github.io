Joan Nwatu

Fourth year CSE PhD Candidate @UMich

I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) program at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor , and a member of the Language and Information Technologies Lab where Professor Rada Mihalcea advises me.

I am interested in improving representation in AI, specifically in computer vision, language and mutlimodal applications.

As much as I enjoy experiencing and learning new things, I love writing about them too. Feel free to check out my blog articles about AI, math, and other exciting topics!
